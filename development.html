Growth & development

More homes require more community.

Development coverage should connect housing growth with roads, servicing, recreation, public space and long-term quality of life.

Development tracker

What to monitor.

🏘️

Housing approvals

Major subdivisions, density changes and projected population growth.

🚧

Infrastructure

Roads, water, wastewater, traffic and capital requirements.

🌳

Public amenities

Parks, trails, courts, gathering spaces and recreation capacity.

The core argument

Growth is not only the number of homes approved.

It is whether the municipality creates the places, services and infrastructure that allow those residents to build a good life.
