Fairgrounds discussion deferred to Q1 2027.

The future of the Port Perry Fairgrounds remains open for further discussion.

Current status

Discussion deferred to Q1 2027.

Council has deferred discussion on the future of the Port Perry Fairgrounds to Q1 2027.

This will leave the future of the property in the hands of the upcoming Council.

The deferral, in theory, leaves additional time for residents, the Port Perry Agricultural Society and other stakeholders to consider what the property should provide for the community in the decades ahead.

The period before 2027 should be used to ask better questions about recreation, public space and the future of the property.
