About The Scugog Scoop

Local decisions. Clear information.

The Scugog Scoop is an independent civic information and opinion project focused on municipal politics and the issues shaping the Township.

Publishing standards

Credibility comes from clear labels.

News / Council update

Verified developments

Grounded in official reports, minutes, agendas, meeting videos or direct statements.

Analysis

What the information may mean

Interpretation based on cited facts, with uncertainty stated clearly.

Opinion

A clearly stated viewpoint

Arguments, recommendations and personal conclusions attributed to the author.

Independence

What this site is—and is not.

The Scugog Scoop is not affiliated with the Township of Scugog, Council, any political party, candidate or municipal department. It should never use Township branding in a way that suggests official status.
