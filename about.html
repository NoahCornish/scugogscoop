Independent coverage for Scugog residents.

The Scugog Scoop is a local civic news and opinion project focused on Council, community issues and the 2026 municipal election.

News

Verified developments

Grounded in official reports, minutes, agendas, meeting video or direct statements.

Council

Public record

Meeting summaries, major votes, deferrals and links to official sources.

Opinion

Clearly stated viewpoints

Arguments and recommendations attributed to the author.

The Scugog Scoop is not affiliated with the Township of Scugog, Council, any political party or any candidate.
