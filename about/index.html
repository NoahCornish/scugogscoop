About

Local issues, made easier to follow.

The Scugog Scoop is an independent civic news and opinion website focused on Council, local decisions and the issues shaping Scugog.

Clear reporting

News stories are based on public records, official documents and direct sources whenever possible.

News and opinion

Reporting and commentary are kept separate and clearly labelled.

Independent

The Scugog Scoop is not affiliated with the Township, Council, any political party or candidate.
